# Future Vision & Roadmap

This document captures the long-term vision and future feature directions for the E-Com Lite platform. These topics represent approved future capabilities that are outside the scope of current implementation milestones.

---

## Theme Support & Aesthetics
* **Dynamic Themes**: Frontend support for Dark Mode and Light Mode, styled with harmonious HSL custom color tokens.
* **Theme Persistence**: Save user theme preferences in local storage or user profiles to maintain consistent presentation across sessions.

---

## Store Deletion & Recovery
* **Administrative Recovery**: A dedicated "Recover Store" dashboard in Platform Admin allowing `SUPER_ADMIN` users to review soft-deleted stores and clear their deletion flags.
* **Audit Trail**: Tracking deletion request history, reasons, and timestamps for administrative records.

---

## Media Uploads & File Management
* **Active Avatar Uploads**: Complete integration of merchant-facing file upload buttons in Store Settings to store images on a content delivery network or server-side media folder.
* **Product Image Uploads**: Enable merchants to drag-and-drop multiple high-resolution images for catalogs, automatically creating media reference lists.

---

## Business Analytics
* **Merchant Dashboards**: Simple charts and summary metrics (total sales, request volumes, inventory alerts) displayed on the Store Dashboard.
* **Platform Overview**: Global analytics for platform administrators to track registration request trends, active store counts, and catalog volume growth.

---

## Notifications System
* **Merchant Alerts**: In-app and email notifications informing store owners when their store requests have been approved or rejected.
* **Platform Alerts**: Action items sent to platform administrators when new store requests are submitted.
